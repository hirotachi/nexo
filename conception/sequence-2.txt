%% Article creation
Note over Contributor: click on "New article"

Contributor ->>+ Client: create article
Client ->> Contributor: request article details
Note over Contributor: enter article details
Client ->> Server: send article details
Server ->>+ Database: store article details
Server ->>+ Client: article stored
Client ->> Contributor: article stored

%% Article editing
Note over Contributor: click on "Edit article"
Contributor ->>+ Client: edit article
Client ->> Contributor: request updates to article
Note over Contributor: enter updates to article
Client ->> Server: send updates to article
Server ->>+ Database: store updates to article
Server ->>+ Client: article updated
Client ->> Contributor: article updated


%% Article deletion
Note over Contributor: click on "Delete article"
Contributor ->>+ Client: delete article
Client ->> Contributor: request article deletion
Note over Contributor: confirm article deletion
Client ->> Server: send article deletion
Server ->>+ Database: delete article
Server ->>+ Client: article deleted
Client ->> Contributor: article deleted
